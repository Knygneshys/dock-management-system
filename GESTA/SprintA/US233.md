# JadeWeserPort — Internal structure improvements

---

## 1. Create a Performance and Innovation Department  
Reports directly to top management. The department would collect data and analyze key performance metrics and help organize improvement projects across departments. Currently, administrative and operational departments operate separately.  
- Will help standardize different future plans across departments.  
- Regular performance reviews will be conducted.  
- Helps identify bottlenecks by analyzing historical data 

 
## 2. Establish continuous improvement systems  
 Hold weekly or monthly meetings to discuss improvement proposals. This helps create better problem identification and feedback systems. Also involves employees in it.   

- Generates ideas for improving performance   
- Creates a better problem identification system  
- Creates a culture of employee involvement   
- More systematic feedback on bottlenecks 

 
## 3. Management information systems upgrade and harmonization  
Connect operational, financial and environmental data by harmonizing and upgrading management information systems.  

- Digital performance dashboards that can be viewed by department managers.  
- Real-time monitoring (e.g. vessel handling time, energy use, delays)  
- Regular data review meetings with senior management 

 
## 4. Cross-departmental performance reviews  
Conduct quarterly reviews where departments assess each other’s contributions to achieving common goals.  

- Highlights inter-departmental relationships and reduces isolation  
- Individual reports replaced by shared reports  
- Shared accountability for shared performance 

 
## 5. Creating a structured feedback system  
Create a feedback system with different clients.  

- Most different improvement suggestions and opportunities come from clients 
- Regular customer feedback  
- Development according to market needs 

 
## 6. Organizing internal workshops and lectures.  
Organize internal workshops and lectures after major projects or disruptions.  

- Helps prevent the recurrence of errors 
- Brings out new ideas for efficiency 
- Improves communication databases
