## 1. Top management - Strategic level 

- **Objective:** 

  By the end of 2030, increase the container turnover of Jade Weser Port by 25%. Using this to expand the infrastructure and strengthen relationships with international maritime transport partners.  

- **SMART analysis:**  

  - **Specific:** Focuses on the growth of container turnover.  
  - **Measurable:** 25% growth in standard containers.  
  - **Achievable:** Supported by the port's capacity and national logistics strategies.  
  - **Relevant:** Linked to long-term competitiveness and economic growth.  
  - **Timed-defined:** The objective must be achieved by the end of 2030  

- **Justification:**
  This objective is suitable for the strategic level, as it has a long-term development direction that is assigned to the port management. The objective addresses the development of infrastructure and market positioning, directing the main achievement targets to investment and partnership decisions. These in turn shape the future competitiveness of the port. 

 

## 2. Middle Management - Tactical Level  

- **Objective:**

  To establish four new intermodal logistics partnerships with rail and inland waterway operators by forth quarter 2026 to improve hinterland connectivity and support revenue growth. 

- **SMART Analysis:**

  - **Specific:** Focuses on the establishment of four specific partnerships. 
  - **Measurable:** Number of contracts to be concluded. 
  - **Achievable:** Implementable within the framework of the expansion of the existing logistics network. 
  - **Relevant:** By improving the efficiency of land transport, it supports strategic revenue growth. 
  - **Timed-defined:** Deadline is forth quarter 2027. 

- **Justification:** 

Suitable for a tactical level objective as the objective must be achieved by the mid-term of the strategic level growth objective. It is driven by middle management. Ensures that operational capabilities are aligned with the strategic objective. As the flow of goods increases, the objective ensures logistical readiness to serve it. 


 
## 3. Low-level Management - Operational Level 

- **Objective:** 

Improve digital tracking systems and process optimization to reduce average container stay time by 10% within 12 months. 

- **SMART Analysis:** 

  - **Specific:** Focuses on reducing container dwell times. 
  - **Measurable:** 10% reduction compared to current indicators 
  - **Achievable:** Achievable through process improvements and technology. 
  - **Relevant:** Improves efficiency, based on the strategic objective 
  - **Timed-defined:** The objective has a deadline of 12 months. 

- **Justification:**

Suitable for the operational level, as it focuses on day-to-day efficiency. The responsible persons are line managers and terminal managers. Fast work organization directly supports the tactical and strategic objective.
